BRM Services

LDTech provides optimized billing solutions to our clients by providing them with Oracle Billing and Revenue Management (BRM) deployment, enhancement, customization and support services.

LDTech with extensive implementation experience of Oracle BRM leads you to success with its billing Best Practices. Whether you need to bring a new offering to market, perform a major system upgrade, or integrate your business systems, LDTech gives customer centric solutions.

BRM revenue generation enables you to deliver services with optimal pricing to your customers. With BRM, service plans can be complex, yet you can change them quickly to respond to changing market dynamic conditions.

BRM customer management tools enable you to continue generating revenue from existing customers by maintaining a positive and proactive relationship.
